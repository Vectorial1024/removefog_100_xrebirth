This mod removes the visual effects of fogs from the game. Specifically, this mod targets the fogs inside Albion [AL], DeVries [DV], Omicron Lyrae [OL] and Maelstrom [MA].

It is well known that fogs in X Rebirth may cause FPS drops. By removing the animations of the fogs, or even removing the fogs themselves, the problem of FPS drops should be less severe.

This mod preserves gaseous resources (hydrogen, ions, plasma) while removing their visual effects. This mod ignores danger zones; danger zones will have their original fog effects.
